About the Text

Brave New World is a stand along novel, still it could be contained within the works of Aldous huxley as his writings had similar themes. His works can be acessed through Huxley's publisher Harper & Brothers where the novel can be purchased in a physical or digital form. Coppies of the work are atainable through a myriad of other ways, such as:

  • Amazon
  • Libraries
  • Book stores
  • pdfs on the internet

However the novel is banned in numerous countries such as Ireland and many states in the US. Nevertheless, the restrictions on this text are lessening and in 50 years it may be availible for all to read in a paper or online format. Thankfully, I and many other scholars have been able to acess and study the text and its connotations.
